Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to visitors, registered users, and members who access AnviPayz through our website, login system, referral pages, wallet pages, task pages, recharge flow, and support channels.

2. Information we collect

We collect information that is reasonably necessary to run AnviPayz and secure member accounts.

  • Account details: Your name, email address, referral code, and optional mobile number.
  • Security data: OTP requests, login timestamps, and verification status.
  • Rewards data: Points earned, tokens converted, and wallet history.
  • Support information: Messages you send to us through support channels.

3. How we use your information

We use collected information to operate, improve, and protect the AnviPayz experience.

  • To manage member accounts and notifications.
  • To calculate balances and referral bonuses.
  • To prevent fraudulent activity and misuse of offers.
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes.
Important: OTP codes are meant only for secure account access. You should never share your OTP with anyone claiming to be from AnviPayz.

4. Data retention

We keep information only for as long as it is reasonably needed for account access, reward management, legal obligations, and security checks.

If you delete your account, we will stop using personal information except where limited retention is necessary for fraud prevention or regulatory compliance.

5. Data security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect stored information. However, no internet-based system can promise absolute security.
